Background

Robur Mining Solutions is a company that was incorporated in Zimbabwe in the year 2021 for the specific need of addressing a specific vision by the Zimbabwean government of yielding a mining anchored economy by 2030.

More importance emanated due to the need within the mining industry to reduce importing and maintaining mining technology which would subsequently lead to short-comings on the support and service side of local mining requirements. ROBUR found itself with a passion for filling this space in the market.

As ROBUR we hence intend on deploying product and technology supported by various OEM suppliers who do not have a footprint in Zimbabwe and hence equipping Robur Mining Solutions' with the specialty to solve Zimbabwe mining problems.
